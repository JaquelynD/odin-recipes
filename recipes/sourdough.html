Sourdough

Description

Always better than storebought, sourdough is a healthy simple option

INGREDIENTS

  • 125g Active soudough starter
  • 454g Flour
  • 325g Spring water
  • 10g Salt

Steps

  1. ensure you have fed your starter and it has doubled in size
  2. Mix the water and sourdough starter in a large bowl
  3. on top of the water/starter mixture, add flour and 10 g of a good quality salt
  4. mix until a shaggy dough forms, cover with a damp kitchen towl and allow to sit for an hour
  5. for the next 4 hours, every half hour, fold the dough and re cover it
  6. dust a clean workspace with flour and place the dough down, stretch it into a rectangle and fold the dough into the shape of the baneton you have, allow the dough to bench rest for a half hour
  7. work the dough again by flattening it, and folding it over on itself until you get a nice shape
  8. place the dough into a dusted banteton (rice flour works very well) cover and place in the fridge over night
  9. the next day, preheat a dutch oven in a 450F oven
  10. take the dough out of the fridge and turn the baneton over onto a piece of parchment paper
  11. using a sharp razor, score the length of the dough creating a long cut in the "skin" of the dough
  12. place the dough into the preheated dutch oven and toss in one icecube, cover and bake for 35 minutes
  13. after 35 minutes, take the cover off the dutch oven and bake for another 15-20 minutes
  14. take bread out of the oven and allow to cool completely before slicing
