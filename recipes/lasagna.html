Lasagna

Description

This recipe has been passed down from Nona to Nona, simple and delicious!

I do not include the measurements of this recipe because you need to use your eyes to measure, its all about layering, you layer until you run out of noodles

INGREDIENTS

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Ground beef
  • CHEESE

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles so they are al dente
  2. While noodles are boiling, cut the onion into a fine dice
  3. Brown the ground beef and add the tomato sauce, set this to the side
  4. In a 4 inch deep pan, put a little bit of tomato sauce in the bottom. You dont need much, just enough to cover the bottom
  5. Now start layering the ingredients together in this order:
    • noodles
    • meat sauce
    • cheese
  6. Once you have completed the layering process, finish by topping with cheese. You may add some basil to the top as well for some added flavor and colour
  7. Cover the pan with foil and bake for 1 hour at 350F
  8. Take the foil off the lasagna and broil on low for 10 minutes
  9. Take the lasagna out of the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving
