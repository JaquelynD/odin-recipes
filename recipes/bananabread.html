Banana Bread

Description

Why do we always buy bananas and then just let them rot on the counter?

The simple answer is that we intended to make banana bread, so lets actually do it!

INGREDIENTS

  • 3 Bananas
  • 1 Egg
  • 1/3c Butter, melted
  • 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  • 1 cup Brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups Flour
  • Pinch Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Mash the bananas into a medium sized bowl
  2. Whisk in the eggs and sugar, once well incorporated slowly add the melted butter until everything comes together
  3. After all the wet ingrediesnts have combined well, add the flour and the baking soda. Mix well.
  4. Bake in a 350F oven for 45-50 minutes, until golden brown on top
